#1. Hike to the Bottom

Fall 2017 was my first experience with The Grand Canyon. I did a day hike 1.5 miles down the South Kaibab Trail to Cedar Ridge. Which is a good turn-a-round point for late starters.

My goal is to return within the next couple of years and stay at the bottom. There are two different lodging options to break up the 14 mile round trip hike. Either staying at Phantom Ranch or Bright Angel Campground.
